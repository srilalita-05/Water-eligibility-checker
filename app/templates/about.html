🎯 Purpose

This AI system analyzes 15 chemical parameters of groundwater to determine if it is suitable for agricultural irrigation. Built with interpretable ML, every prediction comes with a clear explanation of why the water was classified the way it was.

🤖 ML Pipeline
  1. Preprocessing: Median imputation + StandardScaler
  2. Models: Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, XGBoost
  3. Tuning: RandomizedSearchCV (30 iter, 5-fold CV)
  4. Selection: Best model by ROC-AUC
  5. Explainability: SHAP TreeExplainer

📊 Dataset
  • Source: Telangana Groundwater Quality Data
  • Samples: ~1,000 water samples across 30+ districts
  • Season: Post-monsoon 2021
  • Target: P.S. (Safe) / MR (Marginal) / U.S. (Unsuitable)
